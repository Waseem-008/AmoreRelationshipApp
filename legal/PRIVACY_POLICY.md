# Privacy Policy for Amora

**Last Updated**: [Date]

## Introduction

Welcome to Amora. We are committed to protecting your privacy and creating a safe space for couples to strengthen their relationships. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address and password (encrypted)
- **Profile Information**: Relationship details provided during intake form
- **Session Data**: Voice recordings, transcripts, and chat messages from daily sessions
- **Reflections**: Your written reflections on weekly exercises
- **Pairing Information**: Connection with your partner

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: Session timestamps, duration, and completion status
- **Device Information**: iOS version, device model
- **Analytics**: App usage patterns (anonymized)

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

1. **Provide Services**: Enable daily AI-guided sessions and weekly exercises
2. **Personalization**: Tailor conversations based on your relationship history
3. **Improve Product**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
4. **Communication**: Send notifications about sessions and exercises
5. **Support**: Help troubleshoot issues and respond to queries

## Data Privacy & Separation

### Personal vs. Shared Data

- **Personal Memories**: Your individual thoughts and feelings are NEVER shared with your partner
- **Shared Themes**: Only high-level, anonymized patterns are identified as shared themes
- **Partner Privacy**: Each partner's session transcripts remain private

### What Your Partner Can See

- Your session completion status
- Weekly exercise reflections (if you choose to share)
- Shared relationship streak

### What Your Partner CANNOT See

- Your session transcripts
- Your personal memories
- Your individual AI conversations
- Your private reflections

### Deletion
